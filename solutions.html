Where Tarid fits in your day‑to‑day operations.

From onboarding to offboarding, Tarid reduces identity surprises and manual clean‑up for modern SMBs.

Onboarding

Instead of manually creating accounts in every tool, Tarid uses your identity providers and policies to drive onboarding. You choose the rules; Tarid coordinates the changes and keeps a record of what was granted and why.

Offboarding

When someone leaves, Tarid helps you ensure their access is removed consistently—from Google or Microsoft through to Slack, GitHub and other SaaS tools. No more relying on memory or scattered checklists.

Identity drift

Over time, accounts and permissions drift away from what your policies say they should be. Tarid reconciles identities across systems, flags mismatches, and gives you a safe way to bring everything back into line.

Governance and audits

Tarid keeps a clear, reviewable trail of identity changes and access decisions. That makes it easier to answer questions from leadership, auditors, or security reviewers—without digging through logs or tickets.
