Security and governance by design.

Tarid builds on the security of your existing identity providers instead of reinventing it.

Built on Google Workspace and Microsoft Entra ID

Tarid doesn’t store passwords or replace your sign‑in flows. It uses standards‑based integrations with your existing identity providers, so authentication remains where it belongs—inside Google and Microsoft.

Minimal data, clear boundaries

Tarid only stores the information it needs to reconcile identities and apply policies. Access changes and workflows are logged for governance, but sensitive secrets stay with your identity providers and SaaS tools.

Designed for review, not black‑box automation

Every automated change can be reviewed, approved, and audited. Human‑in‑the‑loop flows and clear activity trails mean you can see what changed, when, and why.
