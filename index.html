Why identity orchestration matters for SMBs

Most growing teams end up with identities scattered across Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Entra ID and a long list of SaaS tools. Accounts don’t match, access is granted ad‑hoc, and offboarding is manual. Tarid gives you a practical way to keep identities aligned, access predictable, and changes auditable— without rebuilding your stack.
