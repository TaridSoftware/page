How Tarid orchestrates identity across your stack.

Tarid sits on top of the identity systems you already trust and coordinates authentication, onboarding, and access control across your SaaS environment.

Three practical pillars

Authenticate

Tarid plugs into Google Workspace and Microsoft Entra ID to coordinate authentication and SSO flows. It doesn’t become your primary directory—it respects your existing sources of truth and uses standards‑based protocols like SAML and OIDC to keep sign‑in simple and predictable.

Onboard

Onboarding and role changes are where drift starts. Tarid turns these into guided workflows: confirm the user, apply the right policies, and provision accounts across connected tools. Approval checkpoints and human‑in‑the‑loop steps keep automation safe and reviewable.

Control

Tarid continuously compares what your policies say should exist with what actually exists. It highlights identity drift, misaligned access, and stale accounts, and gives you a clear way to review and correct them—without scripting or manual audits.

Connect the tools you already run on
